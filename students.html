Students

Prospective students

I welcome conversations with students interested in thesis projects or research in high-dimensional statistics, dependent data, functional data analysis, and probability theory for modern statistical methods.

Research Topics

Topics that currently fit especially well include high-dimensional time series, functional data analysis, long-range dependence, change-point analysis, extremes, and statistical methods for dependent or nonstationary data.

Students interested in mathematically rigorous statistical methods and applications to complex data are especially encouraged to get in touch.

When Contacting Me

Please include a brief description of your mathematical and statistical background, your research interests, and any relevant coursework or previous projects.
